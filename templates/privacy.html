Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 18, 2026

1. Data We Collect

Spendly collects information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account Information: Your email address and password when you create an account.
  • Expense Data: Transaction details you enter, including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions.
  • Usage Information: How you interact with our service to help us improve functionality.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve Spendly services
  • Process your transactions and display your expense history
  • Send you technical notices and support messages
  • Respond to your comments and questions

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely in our database. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly may use third-party services for hosting and analytics. These third parties have access to your personal information only to perform specific tasks on our behalf and are obligated to protect your information.

We do not share your financial data with third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your data at any time

6. Children's Privacy

Spendly is not intended for children under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the application.
